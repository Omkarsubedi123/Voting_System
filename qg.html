Quick Guide

Poll Minds – Lion’s Club Voting System

🗳️ Welcome to Poll Minds, the secure and easy-to-use voting platform designed exclusively for Lion’s Club members. Follow this quick guide to cast your vote with confidence.

✅ Step 1: Login
Visit the official Poll Minds website or portal.
Enter your registered email/username and password.
Click “Login” to access your dashboard.

🔒 Tip: Keep your login details secure. Do not share them with anyone.

🗂️ Step 2: View Ongoing Elections
After logging in, you’ll see a list of active elections.
Click on the election title to view candidates or choices, along with their brief profiles or descriptions.

🗳️ Step 3: Cast Your Vote
Select your preferred candidate(s) or option(s) according to the voting rules.
Click “Submit Vote” to confirm your selection.

⚠️ Note: Once submitted, your vote cannot be changed.

🔐 Step 4: Confirmation
You will receive a vote confirmation message on the screen.
A confirmation email or receipt may also be sent to your registered address (if enabled).

🧾 Step 5: Logout
Always click “Logout” after voting, especially on shared devices.

📞 Need Help?
If you experience any issues, contact your Lion’s Club election officer or Poll Minds support team for assistance.
