Greenwood High Holds Student Government Elections

April 21, 2025

Greenwood High concluded its annual student government elections with a record turnout. The new council promises to bring fresh ideas for campus sustainability and student engagement. This year saw the introduction of digital ballots powered by VoteSystem, making voting easier and more secure than ever. Elections officer Ms. Bennett said, "We're thrilled with the transparency and participation."

Digital Voting Triples Participation at Crestview School

April 15, 2025

Crestview School reported three times higher participation in this year’s student council elections, thanks to the VoteSystem online platform. Students praised the ease of voting from home or school. Principal Thomas remarked, “The platform’s real-time results helped maintain excitement and transparency throughout the process!”

VoteSystem Announced for District Board Nomination Polls

April 10, 2025

The school district will use VoteSystem for upcoming Board of Education nomination polls. Officials hope for increased trust and reduced manual errors. Parents will also be able to cast votes this year, a move welcomed by the PTA. Expect detailed guidance sent out next week for secure access.
